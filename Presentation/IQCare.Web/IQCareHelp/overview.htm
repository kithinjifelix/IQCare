IQCare has two major components that make up the entire application of IQCare

 

1) IQCare (browser based data entry application)

•IQCare application configuration

•HIV Care Data entry

•Pre-defined reports and custom reports

2) IQCare Management (Windows based form configuration)

•Form builder technical areas, fields and forms

 

Both parts of the IQCare make up a comprehensive application that is used for managing patients and collecting clean and accurate data for further analysis and program improvement.
