Business Rule Definitions

  

Each field display type can be associated with one or more business rule. These rules are additive, meaning that all of the selected rules will fire either before the form is rendered or at the time the form is saved. See Display Types and Business Rules to see which rules are associated with a field display type.

Lab Selection

 

•If the patient's age is greater than or equal to a numeric value (integer age) AND strictly less then a numeric value (integer or decimal maximum age) then enable the field.

•You must enter both range fields.

•For patients >= 18 years (adults), use a min age of 18 and a max age of 110 years (a number arbitrarily large)

•For patient <18 (children), use a min age of 0 and a max age of 18 years.

•If this business rule is not checked, then the field is enabled for a patient of any age.

•The business rule is fired upon rendering the form.
