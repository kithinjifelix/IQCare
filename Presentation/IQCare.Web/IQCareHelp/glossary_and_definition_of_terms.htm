Term/Acronym

Description

AIDSRelief Project

A US consortium of partners to support antiretroviral therapy (www.aidsrelief.org) including these members:CRS, IMA, CMMB, IHV, FG

CDC

Center for Disease Control

CMMB

Catholic Medical Mission Board

CRS

Catholic Relief Services

CTC, CTC1, CTC2, CTC3

CTC stands for Care and Treatment Clinic

The CTC set of forms are from the Tanzanian -- National HIV Care and Treatment Programme

oCTC1 is the patient-held ID card that has some key data and next appoint information

oCTC2 is the patient record form which is displayed in a register format.;

oCTC3 are the monthly reports required by the Tanzanian MoH

CTCT

In field technical coordinating team for each country (Country Technical Coordinating Team)

FG

Futures Group

Responsible for strategic information initiatives and reporting

HRSA

IHV

Responsible for lab and clinical training
